WHAT MAKES A PERFECT BAR PERFECT?
Fresh is best! Our recipes start in the Keith family kitchen, using fresh ingredients as close to nature as we can find. We make the highest quality food that’s so real, it’s best kept in the fridge.


We use foods that have not been genetically modified and test our finished products for GMOs to ensure that all of our bars are non-GMO.


Sweetened with organic honey, a complex carbohydrate that will ensure your energy levels last. And our vegan flavor uses low glycemic organic dates as the sweetener.


All of our bars are made with high-quality whole food protein — essential for muscle repair and recovery.


Over 20 nutrient-dense organic whole foods provide a good source of vitamins and minerals on-the-go. Plus, with ingredients such as kale, spinach, alfalfa and celery, you will finally be able to tell your mother that you’re eating your vegetables!


Every Perfect Bar flavor recipe is certified by the Orthodox Union, the most trusted kosher certification group in the United States.


We are committed to being gluten-free and are proud to provide products to our community that fit within this diet preference.
